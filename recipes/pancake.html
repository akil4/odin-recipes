Pancakes

Description

Fluffy pancakes made with simple ingredients. Great for a quick breakfast or weekend brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or oil

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter together.
  3. Combine: Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix gently until just combined (do not overmix).
  4. Cook: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles appear on top, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Stack and serve warm with syrup, butter, or fruit.
